Project #7 Data Portrait

Reflection

I collected my data by recording the hold color and level of the problems I did on the day I went indoor bouldering gym. Each square color represents a hold color and the square's outline color represents a level. I portrait the climbing data using only squares and colors. Mauve = level 0 Blue = level 1 Green = level 2 Brown = level 3 Yellow = level 4 Red = level 5
